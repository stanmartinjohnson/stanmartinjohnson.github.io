mary of egypt

a comprehensive digital archive of the hagiographies of st. mary of egypt

about this project

This project compiles and digitizes various accounts of St. Mary of Egypt, a 5th-century ascetic whose story became one of the most popular hagiographies in medieval Christianity. The texts preserved here include Latin accounts from Petrus de Natalibus' Catalogus Sanctorum et Gestorum Eorum (14th century).

The materials available include my English translation from the Latin, a Unicode digitization of the manuscript for scholarly reference, and the original manuscript source.

project goals

This archive aims to make these texts more accessible to scholars and general readers, contributing to the broader project of documenting Mary of Egypt's literary tradition. This work builds upon and complements the research of Florian Janner and other scholars in the field.
